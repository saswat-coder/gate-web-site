A Legacy of Technical Excellence

Gandhi Academy of Technology and Engineering began its journey in 2009 at Berhampur, the Silk City of Odisha. Managed by the Tarini Educational Trust, GATE is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to BPUT, Rourkela and SCTE & VT, Bhubaneswar.

The Institute works with a mission to provide quality education of international standards for producing technocrats and future leaders — in a disciplined, conducive environment as part of our social commitment to promote education globally.

GATE holds NBA Accreditation for Mechanical Engineering (UG), Civil Engineering and Electrical Engineering (Diploma), and is NAAC graded.
