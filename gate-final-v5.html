A Legacy of Technical Excellence

Gandhi Academy of Technology and Engineering began its journey in 2009 at Berhampur, the Silk City of Odisha. Managed by the Tarini Educational Trust, GATE is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to BPUT, Rourkela and SCTE & VT, Bhubaneswar.

The Institute works with a mission to provide quality education of international standards — producing technocrats and future leaders in a disciplined, conducive environment.

GATE holds NBA Accreditation for Mechanical Engineering (UG), Civil Engineering and Electrical Engineering (Diploma), and is NAAC 'A' Grade accredited.

About GATE

Gandhi Academy of Technology and Engineering

Gandhi Academy of Technology and Engineering began its journey in 2009 at Berhampur, the Silk City of Odisha. Managed by the Tarini Educational Trust, GATE is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to BPUT, Rourkela and SCTE & VT, Bhubaneswar.

Our Mission

To provide quality education of international standards for producing technocrats and future leaders in a disciplined, conducive environment.

Accreditations

  • NBA Accreditation – Mechanical Engineering (UG)
  • NBA Accreditation – Civil Engineering (Diploma)
  • NBA Accreditation – Electrical Engineering (Diploma)
  • NAAC 'A' Grade
  • AICTE Approved · BPUT Affiliated · SCTE&VT Affiliated
Academics

B.Tech Programs

GATE offers B.Tech programs across 8 specializations, approved by AICTE and affiliated to BPUT, Rourkela.

Specializations

  • Computer Science & Engineering — 180 Seats
  • CSE (Artificial Intelligence) — 60 Seats
  • CSE (Data Science) — 60 Seats
  • Mechanical Engineering — 90 Seats
  • Civil Engineering — 60 Seats
  • Electrical Engineering — 60 Seats
  • Electronics & Communication — 30 Seats
  • Mining Engineering — 60 Seats

Duration & Eligibility

4 Years (8 Semesters) · 10+2 with PCM · Admission through JEE/OJEE/Direct.

Academics

Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) at GATE is constituted as per NAAC guidelines to develop a system for conscious, consistent and catalytic improvement in the overall performance of the institution.

Objectives

  • To develop a quality culture in the institution
  • To ensure timely, efficient and progressive performance of academic, administrative and financial tasks
  • To ensure equitable access to and affordability of academic programmes
  • To optimize and integrate modern methods of teaching and learning

IQAC Activities

  • Annual Quality Assurance Reports (AQAR)
  • Academic and Administrative Audit
  • Feedback collection from students, parents and employers
  • Faculty Development Programmes (FDP)
  • Workshops on teaching-learning methodologies

Contact

For queries related to IQAC, contact: principal@gate.ac.in

Alumni Association

The GATE Alumni Association connects graduates with the institution and with each other, fostering a lifelong bond of learning, networking and mutual support.

About the Association

GATE has a growing community of alumni spread across India and abroad, contributing to industry, academia and public service. The Alumni Association was formed to maintain and strengthen this connect.

Objectives

  • Maintain a database of GATE graduates
  • Facilitate interaction between alumni and current students
  • Support placement and internship opportunities
  • Organize alumni meets and networking events
  • Encourage alumni contribution to institutional development

Alumni Meet

Annual Alumni Meet is organized every year on the occasion of the Institute's Foundation Day. Alumni are invited to share their industry experiences with current students.

Student Grievance Redressal Cell

The Student Grievance Redressal Committee (SGRC) at GATE is constituted to address and resolve grievances of students in a fair, transparent and time-bound manner.

Scope

  • Academic grievances (examination, evaluation, attendance)
  • Administrative grievances (fee, admission, documents)
  • Infrastructure and facility complaints
  • Hostel-related grievances

How to Lodge a Grievance

  • Online: gate.grievanceportals.com
  • Email: principal@gate.ac.in
  • Written complaint to the Principal's office

Equal Opportunity Cell

GATE has an Equal Opportunity Cell to ensure that students from all backgrounds, including SC/ST/OBC, economically disadvantaged groups and differently-abled students, receive equal access to education and facilities.

Internal Complaint Committee (ICC)

GATE has a duly constituted Internal Complaint Committee under the Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal of Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace Act, 2013.

Information Corner

RTI Information

As per the Right to Information Act, 2005, GATE designates a Public Information Officer (PIO) and Appellate Authority for handling RTI requests.

Central Public Information Officer (CPIO)

  • Name: The Principal, GATE Berhampur
  • Address: Golanthara, Berhampur, Ganjam, Odisha — 761008
  • Email: principal@gate.ac.in
  • Phone: +91 9337753377

How to File an RTI Request

  • Submit written application to the CPIO with Rs. 10/- application fee
  • Response provided within 30 days of receipt
  • First appeal may be filed with the Appellate Authority within 30 days of receipt of reply

Appellate Authority

The Appellate Authority is the Secretary, Tarini Educational Trust, Berhampur, Odisha.
